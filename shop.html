PAWFECT BUDDY

The “Pawfect buddy is a web-based application wherein the welfare of pet animals is taken care of, And help in find Strays and breed Dog for adoption near you .It is a place where stray, lost, abandoned, or surrendered pets are severed. Each pet requires special care and attention which is fulfilled in the organization. The application allows the user to locate strays or lost pet dog and adopt a pet, volunteer towards the pet, purchase the products that are related to pets and donate the desired amount to the organization. The user of the application can work towards a pet as a volunteer and also he/she can adopt the pet from the application also can send photos and information of a dog to get adopte via mail to the admin. The application contains pets’ related products such as food, grooming products, etc. for purchase. The application also provides guidelines to the user regarding the health care of pets, how the pets need to be nurtured, how they have to be taken care of after being adopted.
